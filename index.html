Welcome!

Welcome to the place where imagination becomes real

Imagine waking to the gentle sounds of distant surf, the eerie echo of howler monkeys and the flutter and song of tropical birds. Imagine opening your eyes and seeing a hallucinatory view of mountains and islands meeting an ocean as blue and bright as the sky and Indigo butterflies floating past. Imagine rising from bed and walking through architectural spaces designed with such care and detail that every view is extraordinary and you feel as if you are truly walking in a world of celestial beauty.


History of the house

Both my landscape architect husband and my backgrounds were strongly influenced by beauty, art, and high quality craftsmanship. I grew up being privileged enough to travel around the world while staying in fabulous places. We fell in love with the natural beauty of Costa Rica the first time we saw it. Finding this spot with its location and views was a dream come true. We wanted to create a legacy of a beautiful restorative place where family and friends can truly retreat.

Despite the ongoing challenges we believe we have accomplished this. One of the blessings for me was using my many years of remodeling experience to create a home from it’s inception. Completing a home where even simple things like quality door knobs were in short supply was daunting. It has been a challenging process. Our creative collaboration with architect Victor Cañas in 2003 was energizing. He truly is one of Costa Rica’s treasures and now a friend of ours. Casa Holmes has won significant awards and won the gold medal in the annual Central American architectural competition. Casa Holmes has been featured in books and magazines. However our biggest reward is in the comments we get from our guests or people’s reactions when they first enter Casa Holmes, We especially enjoy return visitors comments on the continual improvements they see.

We follow one of my father’s favorite sayings: “Leave the place where you live better than how you found it.“ We hope the creation of Casa Holmes, along with our respect for Costa Rica’s resources and natural beauty, has done this.
